About Me

I'm Aashiq, a Master's student in Cyber Security at the University of Dubai. My thesis focuses on enhancing digital image forensics using deep learning, watermarking, and Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI). I'm actively involved in research and publication progress from IEEE and ASET conferences.

My research interests include cyber-physical systems, machine learning, digital image forensics, deep learning, and explainable AI. In addition, I have strong interest and practical experience in ethical hacking and penetration testing.
